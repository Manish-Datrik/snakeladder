Snake and Ladders Game https://snakeorladder.netlify.app/

I built this game as a simple digital version of the classic Snake and Ladders board game. The goal was to create something fun, interactive, and easy to play directly in the browser. Players roll a dice and move across a 100-square board filled with snakes and ladders. It’s a light and entertaining game that works well for quick matches with friends.

How to play

Choose your challenge:

2 Player Mode:
Two players take turns rolling the dice and moving across the board. Landing on a ladder moves you up, while landing on a snake sends you down. The first player to reach 100 wins.

3 Player Mode:
Three players compete on the same board. Each player rolls the dice during their turn and moves forward based on the number rolled.

4 Player Mode:
Four players can play together, taking turns rolling the dice and moving across the board until someone reaches the final square.

The Goal

The objective is to reach square 100 before the other players. Ladders help players climb higher on the board, while snakes bring them back down.

Why I made this

I wanted to recreate a classic board game using simple web technologies while practicing game logic and turn-based mechanics.
